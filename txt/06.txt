Telegrams:
GRAYSHOTT. 
Undershaw,
Hinhead,
Haslemere.
Feb 3.1900.

 Dear Sir
I have the greatest pleasure
in acknowledging & accepting the
honour done me by the Irvine 
Burns Club of which I 
shall be proud to call myself 
Honorary Member.
Pray convey my thanks to 
the members

& believe me
Yours faithfully
Conan Doyle


This letter was written by Arthur Conan Doyle on 3 february 1900 from Undershaw, Hindhead, Haslemere, Surrey, to the Irvine Burns Club.
